Key features

EOL Early Warning

We flag components approaching end-of-life 12–18 months out, with enough runway to qualify an alternate before your redesign becomes an emergency.

Tariff & Trade Exposure

We map every component to its HTS code and flag tariff rate changes, Section 301 exposure, and Entity List restrictions the day they change.

Supplier Concentration Risk

Single-source dependencies, China concentration, and TSMC-dependent components scored and flagged before they become leverage someone else holds over you.

When we flag a risk, we suggest pin-compatible alternates with live Octopart availability data. Not just a warning, but a path forward.
